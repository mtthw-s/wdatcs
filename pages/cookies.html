Ingredients

Cooking spray

2 large eggs

1 stick melted butter

2 tbsp heavy cream

2 tsp. vanilla extract

2 3/4 cup almond flour

1/4 tsp. salt

1/4 cup granulated (sugarfree) sugar

3/4 c. dark chocolate chips

Directions

These cookies have a distinctive nutty flavor. Also, they don't flatten out like regular cookies. Whatever shape you lay them on the tray will be the shape they come out of the oven in. Keep that in mind, as thicker cookies could take longer to bake.

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F. In a large bowl, whisk the egg with the butter, heavy cream, and vanilla. Stir in the almond flour, salt, and sugar.
  2. Fold the chocolate chips into the cookie batter. Form the batter into 1" balls and arrange 3" apart on parchment lined baking sheets. Flatten the balls with the bottom of a glass that has been lightly greased with cooking spray.
  3. Bake until the cookies are golden, about 17 to 19 minutes.
  4. Enjoy
